TechStart E-learning Platform

Education Technology

Challenge

TechStart needed to transition their traditional in-person coding bootcamp to a fully online learning platform during the global pandemic, while maintaining their high standards of interactive learning and student engagement.

Solution

Developed a custom learning management system featuring:

  • Real-time collaborative coding environments
  • Interactive video lessons with progress tracking
  • AI-powered student performance analytics
  • Virtual mentorship matching system

Results

  • 95% student satisfaction rate
  • 40% increase in course completion rates
  • 60% reduction in administrative overhead
  • Successfully scaled from 100 to 1000 students in 6 months
